# Upgrade zabbix from 2.4 to 3.0
The instructions are laid out in:
https://www.zabbix.com/documentation/3.0/manual/installation/upgrade

Also version specific notes are here:

https://www.zabbix.com/documentation/2.4/manual/installation/upgrade_notes_247

Nothing note-worthy so I decided to go ahead with it.
